Teethbrushing Tutorial

Bad breath ruining your social life and making your
friends and colleagues resent you? There is a simple solution!

BRUSHING YOUR TEETH!

Here's what you need:

  • One toothbrush
  • Toothpaste
  • A source of water (for rinsing)

"Now that I have gathered all of these items, what is the procedure to cleaning my teeth?"

Follow these steps closely:

  1. Apply toothpaste to toothbrush
  2. Insert toothpasted toothbrush into your mouth
  3. Firmly grasping the handle, drag the bristly part of the brush
    over your teeth until white foam accumulates
  4. Continue until you have brushed all of your teeth, front and back
  5. Spit white foam out, preferably into the sink
  6. Rinse mouth and brush with water
